Double The interest rate percentage of the loan used to calculate the Interest Payment of the Instalment reflected on the Amortization Schedule for the loan. The Interest Rate applying for the Arrangement Mortgage Loan is derived from the Funding Arrangement. Depending on the type of Mortgage, the Interest Rate at Arrangement Mortgage Loan (1) is reflecting the Interest Rate at Arrangement Funding (Fastforrentede obligationslån) (2) is calculated when Mortgage Bond Prices for a new Funding Arrangement are known (Kontantlån/Tilpasningslån) (3) is calculated when a new reference Interest Rate is published (Variabeltforrentede obligationslån) Danish: Renteprocent format: double
principalAmountOutstanding
Amount Principal Amount Outstanding states the amount left of the Principal Amount at the Latest Activity Date. The latest Principal Amount Outstanding is e.g. used to calculate the following Instalment amounts like Interest Payment, Interst Margin Payment, and Principal Payment. When used in context Mortgage, Principal Amount Outstanding is the remaining amount by a given date that the Borrower is obliged to pay back. This Principal Amount Outstanding is in legacy refered to as - 'Kontantrestgæld' for products of type 'Kontantlån' or 'Tilpasningslån' - 'Restgæld' for products of type 'Fastforrentet Obligationslån'. In these cases, the amount will be the same as Principal Amount Outstanding in context Funding. Danish: Restgæld
principalAmount

When used in Arrangement Mortgage Loan, Principal Amount is the amount that the Borrower is obliged to pay back. This Principal Amount is in legacy refered to as - 'Kontanthovedstol' for products of type 'Kontantlån' or 'Tilpasningslån' - 'Hovedstol' for products of type 'Fastforrentet Obligationslån'. In these cases, the amount will be the same as Principal Amount in Arrangement Funding. Danish: Hovedstol

PaymentPattern - Up

type
String The payment pattern type - 'amortized': Identifies a payment which is made by periodic transfers to a sinking fund. For example 'annuitet amortisering' with equal repayment amount where the interest payment is a decreasing portion of each payment. - 'fixed-principal': Identifies a payment which is made to reduce the principal outstanding balance by a fixed payment amount. For example serial loan repayment with equal principal amounts repaid i the payment period. - 'interest-with-deferred-principal ': Identifies a payment which is applied to the interest due for the period, with repayment of principal deferred until a later date or when demanded, or utilizing the remainder of funds above the interest amount due to apply towards the principal payment. For example a 'stående lån' with payment of principal at the end of loan period.
Enum:
amortized
fixed-principal
interest-with-deferred-principal
startDate
date The start date (date inclusive) of the period for the given payment pattern format: date
endDate
date The end date (date inclusive) of the period for the given payment pattern format: date
